Archinet gives you freedom
Our story's simple: our founder wanted to try out an electric skateboard one day, but didn't want to pay full price for one without being satisfied that it was what he wanted. He hunted online for a way to rent one, but to no avail. Through some more research, it was apparent that there was no major online peer to peer rental platform that allows people to both save and make money. This came across as a problem to us, so we set out to fix it.
Archinet lets you list anything you own except for automotive vehicles and real estate. Everything else is fair game. We want people to have access to items they need or want, without having to worry about the price.
We value opporunity beyond everything else. We aim to assure that people are able to gain new experiences while exploring anything and everything they find intriguing.
